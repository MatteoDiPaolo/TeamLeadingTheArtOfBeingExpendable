- A jack of all trades

The goal

  • Being expendable
  • Cohesive and autonomous team
  • Having the team spinning forever
- not being there in the future will not be a problem
- team's dynamics should flow and arise spountaneosuly
- inner dynamics between team members
- outer dynamics between team and client

A jack of all trades

“ master of none though oftentimes better than master of one ”
  • Tech lead
  • Architect
  • DevOps
  • Project manager
  • Product owner
  • Senior dev
  • Mentor
  • ...
- depending on the project needs - you don't actually need to know everything about every role - you have to unblock things for the team

The unblocker

  • Issues
  • Doubts
  • Fires
  • Requirements
  • Risks
  • Meetings
  • Chats
  • Pairings
  • Deadlines
  • ...
- in order to be ready to unblock you have to be always unblocked (tasks assignation) - follow any communication thread even if you are not tagged in it - go to person, but not forever - minimize the possiblity to have blockers

Architecture diagram

clean and tidy house

Boards and tickets
- on top of boards, up to date picture of team working status - detailed tickets, PO / client must be educated to it as any other team members - leave devs possiblity to create their own subtasks

delegating and guarding

“ Concealed within his fortress, the lord of Mordor sees all. ”
- review chats in order to correct, punctualize things - review code and PRs - review infrastructure usage - review task development process

team shape matters

actions and words matter

  • Be an example
  • Assertive, transparent and backed by facts communication
- being an example goes a long way, you give it all, you receive it all back - be careful what you say, it has a big impact (both positive and negative) - fernando, detailed summary of his work - dani, too much harsh feedback - laura, david drunk

To bear in mind

“ I hate losing more than I even wanna win. ”
- success is on the team, failure is on you
